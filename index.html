How it works

  1. In game: open your map at the spot you want to remember and take a screenshot Win+Shift+S works great — snip just the map area.
  2. Here: press Ctrl+V and choose Map screenshot. The site finds where it belongs, reveals exactly the rooms shown in your screenshot, and drops a pin on your player marker — all automatic. If it isn't confident enough it reveals nothing and asks for a wider screenshot. Ctrl+Z undoes the last paste.
  3. In game: screenshot what's actually there (the door, the NPC, the ledge you can't reach) and just paste it — it attaches to the new pin automatically, or hit Skip.
  4. Pick a category (locked door, needs ability, vendor…), write a short note, and drag the pin if it isn't exactly on the spot. Hovering the pin now shows your screenshot.
  5. When you finally come back and deal with it — open the pin and hit ✓ done.
  6. Zoom your in-game map all the way out, screenshot it and paste as Full map to update everything you've explored in one go.

Clear map erases only the revealed map (your pins, their pictures and notes all stay) — handy for rebuilding the map from fresh screenshots. Reset erases everything.

Everything is stored in this browser only — nothing is uploaded. Use Export now and then to keep a backup file.
