How it works

  1. Snip your in-game map. Open the map, press Shift + Win + S, and drag a box around the part you want. Then paste it here with Ctrl+V.
  2. Keep at least one area name in the shot. The site reads the printed area name to work out where your screenshot belongs — if no name is visible, it can't place it.
  3. Equip the Compass for automatic pins. With the Compass, your player marker shows on the map, so a pin drops on your exact spot automatically. Without it the area is still revealed — you just drag the pin into place yourself.
  4. Pick a category, add a note and a picture of the spot, and you're done. Hovering the pin shows your screenshot; open it and hit ✓ done once you've dealt with it.
  5. Zoomed all the way out, screenshot the whole map and paste as Update your map to refresh everything at once. Ctrl+Z undoes the last paste.

Clear map erases only the revealed map (pins, pictures and notes stay). Reset erases everything. Everything is stored in this browser only — use Export now and then for a backup.
